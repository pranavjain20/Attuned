# Day 3 — WHOOP Personal Intelligence

Built the entire WHOOP intelligence layer: personal baselines (HRV, RHR, sleep stages), 7-day trend slopes, sleep deficit analysis, and a 7-state composite classifier. 825 recovery days of real data validated.

Key engineering decisions:
- Recovery-first classifier (replaced weighted scoring) — trust WHOOP's recovery score as primary signal, use individual metrics to determine HOW to help, not WHETHER it's bad
- Multi-sleep-per-date handling — WHOOP stores naps as separate records, must group by date before aggregating
- Baseline window excludes today, trend window includes it — evaluating today AGAINST the baseline, not including it in the baseline

Redesigned classifier twice. Renamed `single_bad_night` → `poor_recovery` across 8 files — state names should capture the emotional reality.

Staff tester audit: 6 HIGH + 9 MEDIUM gaps found, all fixed. Staff auditor: 1 MUST FIX + 5 SHOULD FIX, all fixed.
